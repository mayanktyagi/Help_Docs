De Novo Assembly Samples Page

The Assembly workflow enables de novo assembly of a reference genome directly from the sequencing reads. Because assembly relies upon significant coverage of the reference genome, this workflow is best suited for the assembly of bacterial genomes (such as E. coli). The assembly process is performed by the Velvet software.

The de novo assembly App Results page provides access to three graphs, described below:

    • Low Percentage Graph
    • High Percentage Graph
    • Clusters Graph

The De Novo Assembly Sample Details Page provides two panes, described below:

    • De Novo Assembly Samples Table
    • Assembly Samples Graph
