Sample Overview Page

The Sample Overview page provides 2 panes:

    • The Sample Details pane gives a summary of the run with a links to launch a custom BaseSpace app on your sample. Clicking on the app name leads to a page with more information about that app, including access permissions.
    Note that running custom apps may incur a charge.
    • The Files pane gives a list of files associated with that sample. You can either look at all FASTQ files, or look at files specific for an app session. See also View Files and Results.
