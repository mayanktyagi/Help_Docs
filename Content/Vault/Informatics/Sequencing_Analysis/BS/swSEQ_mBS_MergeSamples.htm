Merge Samples

What is it

A method to combine (merge) samples.

When to use it

When you want to merge the data from two different sequencing runs on the same sample.

How to use it

    1 Click the Projects icon.
    2 Click the desired project.
    3 From the Project Overview page click the Samples icon to go to the Sample List.
    4 Select the checkboxes of the samples you want to combine.
    5 Click the Combine button in the top right corner.
    6 Click the Combine button in the pop-up screen.
